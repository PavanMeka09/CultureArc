Vinča Idol

Period

Neolithic Era

Culture

Vinča Culture

Region of Origin

Danube River Basin

Materials

Terracotta, Pigment

This terracotta figurine is a prime example of the Vinča culture's sophisticated artistry. Characterized by its abstract facial features and geometric incisions, it likely held significant ritual or spiritual importance. The artifact provides invaluable insight into the symbolic world of one of Europe's earliest and most influential Neolithic societies.
